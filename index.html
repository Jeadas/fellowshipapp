Loadouts (gear combinations)

Keep several viable gear combinations — e.g. Drakheim Chest + off-piece Legs, or the reverse. Your one BiS target applies to all of them, and the Plan tab gives a plan for each (closest-to-finished first).

Slot roles & dungeon sources

For the active loadout: Weapon & both Relics are fixed (drop anywhere). Of the other 11, assign exactly 1 Legendary, 8 Set pieces, 2 Free. In Drops from, toggle the dungeon(s) each Set or Free piece can drop from (set per slot, right here — pick several if it can come from more than one).

Sets

Just the set names (the set bonus those pieces carry). The dungeon each piece drops from is set per slot in the loadout above.

Dungeons

Where bases drop. Used to tell you which dungeon to farm for missing pieces.

Weapon trait tree

Separate from the weapon's 2 modifier slots: grants +1 rank to one Major, two Heroic and two Defensive traits (freely re-rollable, off the 27-mod budget).

Modeling assumptions

Build your BiS target

Pick a modifier type, then the exact modifier, then how many ranks (modifier slots) you want. Gem essence ranks = ×100 Gem Power. Each rank consumes one of your 27 modifier slots.

Modifier budget

Add an owned item

Enter items sitting in your stash that are not yet maxed. Pick the slot, its current rarity, and each customizable modifier it currently has, in slot order. Skip the locked 1st slot on set pieces (set bonus), Weapons / Relics (ability) and the necklace attunement — only enter the mods you can actually re-roll. The planner re-checks your path on every add.
