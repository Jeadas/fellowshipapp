Fellowship S3 · BiS Gear Planner

Loot 2.0 itemization — plan a path from your stash to your dream build

Slot roles

14 worn items. Weapon & both Relics are fixed (1st mod = their ability). Of the other 11, assign exactly 1 Legendary, 8 Set pieces, and 2 Free items (the free items are the only ones that can carry a distinct 3rd modifier).

Sets

Define your sets and tag which slots belong to them (cosmetic for planning — a set piece always burns its 1st mod on the set bonus).

Weapon trait tree

Separate from the weapon's 2 modifier slots: grants +1 rank to one Major, two Heroic and two Defensive traits (freely re-rollable, off the 27-mod budget).

Modeling assumptions

Build your BiS target

Pick a modifier type, then the exact modifier, then how many ranks (modifier slots) you want. Gem essence ranks = ×100 Gem Power. Each rank consumes one of your 27 modifier slots.

Modifier budget

Add an owned item

Enter items sitting in your stash that are not yet maxed. Pick the slot, its current rarity, and each customizable modifier it currently has, in slot order. Skip the locked 1st slot on set pieces (set bonus), Weapons / Relics (ability) and the necklace attunement — only enter the mods you can actually re-roll. The planner re-checks your path on every add.
